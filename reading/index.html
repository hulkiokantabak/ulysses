The reading — the primary record

The project is built out of one first cover-to-cover reading of Ulysses, 7–17 July 2026, in sixteen logged sessions. Every dimension descends from page-anchored moments in this record; it is the spine the rest of the site hangs from.

  • The Reading Companion — the whole read, verbatim, in order: 117 pages of questions and answers.
  • The questions only — the 17-page set separated from its answers, sent to an independent second build.
  • Four Odysseys — Homer, Wilson, Nolan, Joyce: the comparative material behind dimension sixteen.
