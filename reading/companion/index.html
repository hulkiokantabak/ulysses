The Reading Companion — the whole read, verbatim

The complete question-and-answer record of the reading: every session, in order, with the questions asked at the exact points of strain and the answers that resolved them. This is the one irreplaceable document in the project — everything else can be rebuilt from it, and it cannot be rebuilt from anything.

Ulysses Reading Companion — the full Q&A of the read (117 pages) (PDF)

Hulki Okan Tabak — the primary record of a first reading of Ulysses, 7–17 July 2026. The reader's questions are reproduced as spoken, dictation slips included (anchor A6).

The record is a PDF (117 pages, under half a megabyte). An earlier version of this page said an HTML edition with addressable questions would arrive with dimension 11, the Difficulty Curve. It did not: dimension 11 built an analysis over this record — where the questions cluster and what kinds they are — not an addressable edition of it. No such edition exists, and none is scheduled. The promise is withdrawn rather than left standing, per anchor A4.
