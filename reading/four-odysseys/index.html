Four Odysseys — Homer, Wilson, Nolan, Joyce

A four-way comparison measured on two axes that are usually collapsed into one: fidelity to Homer's plot and genre, and empathetic or interior orientation. This is the source material for dimension sixteen, and it supplies the analytic frame for the Concordance of Failures.
