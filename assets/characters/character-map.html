Everyone in Ulysses,
and the day they cross

Dublin, Thursday 16 June 1904. Four circles of cast, drawn inside a ring of the book's eighteen hours. Tap an hour to see who stands where; tap a person to see who they are and what binds them.

The outer ring is the day. Joyce gave every episode an hour except the last — Penelope has no hour in his own schema, so its mark sits outside the ring, unfilled. The three dotted arcs join the episodes that happen at the same time: the book's first three hours are lived twice, once by Stephen and once by Bloom.
