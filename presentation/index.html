The presentation — the Madrid-logic decks

The project ends in a two-deck presentation: an audience deck of image cards only, and a presenter deck with the image card plus the material to speak from. Ten facts and three questions per topic; the questions must not echo the facts. The format was ratified on the Paseo del Arte decks — an earlier two-deck project of the author's on Madrid's museum mile, the Prado and the Reina Sofía, which is why the project calls it the Madrid logic — and is settled; it is specified in full in the method.

No deck has been built yet. Nothing in the presentation register exists at this point, and this page says so rather than showing placeholders.

By the register budget, decks will come from: The Object Museum · The Women's Dublin · The Governed City · The Concordance of Failures · The Difficulty Curve · Ulysses in Turkish · The Atlas of One Day · Four Odysseys. The current state of that register is always visible at the presentations index.
