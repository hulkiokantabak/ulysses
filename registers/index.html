By register — the second entrance

Every dimension gets output in up to four registers. They are distinct products, not four exports of one file. This index enters the grid from the register edge: everything of one kind, across the whole project. It is generated from the same data as the dimension index — the two entrances cannot drift apart.

  • HTML — Single-file, self-contained, interactive where interaction earns its place; doubles as the build material for the website.
  • PDF reporting — The durable, portable, offline register — hyperlinked and cross-linked.
  • Presentation — The Madrid logic: an audience deck and a presenter deck, ten facts and three questions per topic.
  • Maps, images and diagrams — Standalone references in their own right, and components integrated into the PDF and HTML packages.
