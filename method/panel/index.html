The advisors and their sessions

Two panels with distinct jurisdictions govern this project. The literary masters judge reading — which dimension deserves attention. The nine advisors judge building — whether the artifact works, whether the link resolves, whether a first reader can use it. They are never merged. The chair proposes; the author ratifies.

  • The nine advisors — the build panel: two technologists, three literary advisors, two historians, two chair's picks, with its routing table and type discipline.
  • The masters session — the adversarial session on which dimension to build next, which produced ten of them and the finding that reorganised them all.
  • Panel session 01 — do we go to Code now? Unanimously yes, with four binding vetoes — one of which the author later overruled, and the record of that stands in the log.
