The Governed City

Dimension 3 · Type I, Inventory (extract a category, enumerate, tabulate) · Build status: built · Panel score: 5

Chair's pick of the Type-I three (anchor A11), completing the inventory cap. Its HTML register is skipped by budget, so the built artifact carries the diagram register — the matrix of instruments across the day. Built to every register it budgets: diagram, presentation, and — 19 July 2026 — the document PDF.

The claim
Dublin is governed everywhere and administered nowhere the reader is taken. The instruments of rule — Castle, police, garrison, courts, the language question — surface across nearly every episode, but almost always as reputation, salute or memory rather than as an act.
The unit
the instrument of rule — Castle, police, barracks, informers, courts, language
What it shows
Thirteen instruments spread thin across the whole day: rule is an ambient hum, not a scene, and the matrix of where each surfaces shows it. It becomes an event only twice — the Citizen's nationalism in Cyclops and the hallucinated trial in Circe — and both times the book has left realism behind. The protagonists never enter a tenement, a barracks or a courtroom (catch C-03); the state reaches them as a passing constable, a viceregal cavalcade, and the English they are made to speak.
