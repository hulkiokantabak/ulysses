Four Odysseys

Dimension 16 · Type IV, Comparative (the book against other versions of itself) · Build status: building · Panel score: not yet scored

This dimension is being built now.

Material in hand: the comparative handoff document under Reading. Supplies dimension 9's analytic frame — refusing plot and refusing register are different acts.

The claim
Fidelity to Homer's plot and interior orientation are two separable axes, usually collapsed into one. Collapsed, the four Odysseys line up on a single scale of faithfulness; separated, they stop lining up at all.
The unit
the work, measured on two axes — fidelity to Homer's plot and genre, and interior orientation (Homer · Wilson 2017 · Nolan 2026 · Joyce 1922)
What it shows
Wilson and Joyce share an anti-mystification impulse — both remove something (archaism; heroic distance) rather than add, while Nolan adds. Wilson de-mystifies the sentence; Nolan de-mystifies the hero but keeps the myth; Joyce discards the myth's events and re-mystifies the ordinary. Three different kinds of empathy — not one scale of it.
